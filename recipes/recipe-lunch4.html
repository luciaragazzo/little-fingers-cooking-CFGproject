FRITTATA

Frittas are great for using up leftover vegetables, especially potatoes. You just use whatever you have left over from supper the night before or the lunchtime roast

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 20
Oven: 35

Main tasks & skills

  • Painting

  • Chopping

  • Whisking

  • Pouring

Allergy information

Egg Avoid
Dairy Use dairy-free milk and leave out cheese
Gluten Not applicable
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • 200g mixed vegetables of your choice. E.g. cooked potatoes, broccoli, courgettes, peas, peppers, cooked carrots

  • 4 eggs

  • 120ml milk

  • 70g grated cheddar cheese

PARENT PREP

  • Grate cheddar cheese

  • Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas 4

  • Lay out: ingredients, oven-proof dish or pan, pastry brush, fork, large mixing bowl, chopping board and table knife

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Brush the oil all over the oven-proof dish with a pastry brush

  2. Break the eggs into the mixing bowl. Add the milk and whisk together with a fork

  3. Cut the vegetables into chunks with the table knife or tear them. Add to the bowl

  4. Add the grated cheese and mix everything together with a fork

  5. Pour the mixture into the oven-proof dish

AND FINALLY

Season and bake int the oven for 30-35 minutes until golden and firm
