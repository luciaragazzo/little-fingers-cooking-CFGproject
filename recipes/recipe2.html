BLUEBERRY YOGHURT MUFFINS

These aren't the fluffiest of muffins, but lack of eggs and the throw-everything-into-a-bowl-and-mix concept is perfect for young toddlers to manage

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 15
Oven: 20

Main tasks & skills

  • Mixing

  • Spooning

  • Counting

Allergy information

Egg Not applicable
Dairy Use dairy-free milk
Gluten Use gluten-free flour and 1/2 a beaten egg
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 3tbsp vegetable oil

  • 4tbsp Greek-style or blueberry yoghurt

  • 1/2 tbsp honey

  • 20g blueberries

  • 65g self-raising flour

  • 1/8 tsp bicarbonate of soda

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas 4

  • Lay out: ingredients, bowl, mixing spoon, 2 tablespoons, 4 cupcake cases and a cupcake tray

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Put the oil, yoghurt, honey and blueberries into the bowl and mix

  2. Add the flour and bicarbonate of soda. Mix until combined

  3. Count out 4 cases into the tray

  4. Use 2 spoons to scrape the mixture into the cases until each is two-thirds full

AND FINALLY

Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until they feel springy on top
