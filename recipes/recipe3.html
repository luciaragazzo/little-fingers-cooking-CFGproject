OVERNIGHT OATS

Easiest made the day before for a grab-from-the-fridge breakfast the next morning. This is a good recipe for early measuring practice

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 10
Chill: 60

Main tasks & skills

  • Measuring

  • Spooning

  • Mixing

Allergy information

Egg Not applicable
Dairy Use dairy-free yoghurt
Gluten Oats usually okay, but check label for potential contamination
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 4 tbsp oats

  • 4 tbsp Greek-style or natural yoghurt

  • 4 tbsp blueberries, frozen or fresh

  • 1/2 tbsp runny honey

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas 4

  • Lay out: ingredients, mixing bowl, mixing spoon, 2 tablespoons, 2 jars with lids

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Put the oats, yoghurt, blueberries and the honey into the bowl

  2. Mix thoroughly

  3. Spoon and scrape half the quantity into one jar and half into the other

  4. Put on the lids

AND FINALLY

Put into the fridge for a minimum of 1 hour
