BAKED PANCAKE

Baked pancake has all the flavour of pancake but without the frying and tossing in a hot pan

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 15
Oven: 15

Main tasks & skills

  • Whisking

  • Rubbing

  • Pouring

  • Decorating

Allergy information

Egg Avoid
Dairy Use dairy-free milk
Gluten Use gluten-free flour
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 50g plain flour

  • 1/8 tsp salt

  • 90ml milk

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 tsp butter

  • Pancake topping of your choice

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F/Gas 6

  • Lay out: ingredients, 20-cm-square cake tin (or ovenproof dish), mixing bowl and hand whisk

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Put the flour and salt into the bowl

  2. Break the eggs into the bowl

  3. Pour in half the milk. Whisk until combined

  4. Add the rest of the milk and whisk again until bubbly

  5. Rub the butter all over the cake tin

  6. Pour the batter into the tin

AND FINALLY

Bake in the oven for 15 minutes until golden and fluffy. Take out of the hot tin and put onto a plate. Then get the children to decorate with their choice of toppings
