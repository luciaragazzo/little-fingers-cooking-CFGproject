MINI SCONES

These mini versions make a great tea-time treat or snack

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 25
Oven: 15

Main tasks & skills

  • Tickling

  • Mixing

  • Squishing

  • Painting

Allergy information

Egg Not applicable
Dairy Use dairy-free spread and milk
Gluten Use gluten-free flour
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 34g butter

  • 130g self-raising flour

  • 1/2 tbsp sugar

  • Tiny pinch salt

  • 35g currants

  • 60ml milk

  • 1tbsp milk for brushing tops

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 190°C/375°F/Gas 5

  • Lay out: ingredients, mixing bowl, mixing spoon, 48-mm round cookie cutter, pastry brush, baking tray, table knife and chopping board

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Cut the butter into little pieces

  2. Put the flour and butter pieces into the bowl. Rub the butter into the flour using fingertips until it looks like breadcrumbs

  3. Add the sugar, currants and a pinch of salt and mix

  4. Add the milk and mix. Bring the mixture together with hands until you have a lump of dough

  5. Tip onto the board. Flatten out the dough with hands to 1.5cm thick

  6. Cut out dough circles with a cutter. Put the shapes on the baking tray and paint the tops with milk using a pastry brush

AND FINALLY

Bake in the oven for 15 minutes until light brown. If you're making a single scone, bake for an extra 5 minutes
