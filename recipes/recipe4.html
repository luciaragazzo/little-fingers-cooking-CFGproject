BAKED EGGS BENEDICT

This is a fun way for young children to make a special breakfast or light meal for the family. Hollandaise can be sharp for little taste buds so you can use mayonnaise instead

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 10
Oven: 15

Main tasks & skills

  • Painting

  • Layering

  • Tearing

Allergy information

Egg Avoid
Dairy Check muffins label
Gluten Check muffins label
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • 4 English muffins

  • 4 eggs

  • 4 slices ham

  • 4 tbsp hollandaise (optional)

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 190°C/375°F/Gas 5

  • Lay out: ingredients, oven-proof dish and pastry brush

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Use a pastry brush to brush oil all over the inside of the dish

  2. Tear the ham into pieces and put all over the bottom of the dish

  3. Tear the muffins into bite-sized pieces and put on top of the ham

  4. Break the eggs on to the muffins

AND FINALLY

Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the egg whites are white and cooked. Serve with a dollop of hollandaise (optional)
