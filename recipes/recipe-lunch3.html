TOMATO SALSA BRUSCHETTA

If your little ones are too young to chop or snip, make the salsa yourself and let them 'paint'/'decorate' the ciabatta with salsa and basil

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 25

Main tasks & skills

  • Chopping

  • Painting

  • Snipping

  • Constructing

Allergy information

Egg Not applicable
Dairy Not applicable
Gluten Use gluten-free ciabatta
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

For tomato salsa:

  • 8 cherry tomatoes

  • 1 spring onion

  • 1/4 small or 1/8 large lime

For bruschetta:

  • 1/2 ciabatta loaf

  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil

  • Basil leaves (optional)

PARENT PREP

  • Slice and toast ciabatta

  • Lay out: ingredients, table knife, chopping bowl, child-safe scissors, bowl, tablespoon and pastry brush

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

For the tomato salsa:

  1. Use the table knife to chop the tomatoes into 8 pieces and put into the bowl

  2. Use the scissors to snip the spring onion into little pieces and add to the bowl

  3. Squeeze over the juice of the lime quarter and mix in

For the bruschetta:

  1. Brush the olive oil over each slice of toast

  2. Spoon a dollop of salsa onto each slice

  3. Tear the basil leaves into little pieces and sprinkle on top
