PITTA CHIPS

This is an easy, quick snack to just serve plain, but these chips are also great to have with either a dip or a bowl of delicious, warming soup

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 10
Oven: 10

Main tasks & skills

  • Tearing

  • Mixing

  • Sprinkling

Allergy information

Egg Not applicable
Dairy Not applicable
Gluten Use gluten-free pittas
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 2 pittas

  • 2 tsp of dried mixed herbs

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

PARENT PREP

  • Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F/Gas 6

  • Lay out: ingredients, child-safe scissors (optional) and baking tray

VARIATIONS

Leftover baked potato skins, tortillas, thinly sliced carrots, parsnips or aubergines

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Open the pittas and cut or tear them into 2-cm pieces

  2. Put the pitta pieces onto the tray

  3. Take a pinch of herbs and sprinkle them over the pitta pieces

  4. Drizzle over the olive oil

  5. Mix, using hands or a spoon, to coat pitta pieces with oil and herbs

AND FINALLY

Bake in the oven for 10 minutes until light brown and crispy
