TUNA MAYO PITTA POCKETS

Prepare them in advance and then warm just before eating. They are also good uncooked for picnics

Cooking time (minutes)

Activity: 15
Oven: 5

Main tasks & skills

  • Mushing

  • Stuffing

  • Mixing

Allergy information

Egg Leave out mayo
Dairy Leave out cheese and check mayo label
Gluten Use gluten-free pittas
Nut Check labels for potential contamination

WHAT YOU NEED

  • 200g tinned tuna

  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise

  • 3 tbsp tinned sweetcorn

  • 70g cheddar cheese

  • 4 pitta halves

PARENT PREP

  • Open tuna and sweetcorn tins and drain off liquid

  • Grate cheddar cheese, or if you have a handled grater let your child do it

  • Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F/Gas 4

  • Lay out: ingredients, mixing bowl, 2 tablespoons, folk and baking tray

ALL TOGETHER NOW!

  1. Scrape the tuna into the mixing bowl

  2. Spoon mayonnaise into the mixing bowl and mush into the tuna with hands or a fork

  3. Add the sweetcorn and mix together

  4. Spoon or use hands to put 2 spoonfuls of tuna mix into each pitta half

  5. Use hands to shove a handful of grated cheese inside each pitta half

  6. Put on the baking tray

AND FINALLY

Warm in the oven for 5 minutes. Eat straight away
